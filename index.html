🧠 How to Play

  1. 🎲 Roll the Dice:
    Click the “ROLL DICE” button to roll. The dice value (2–6) will be added to your current score.
  2. ⛔ Watch out for 1:
    If you roll a 1, your current round score is lost, and your turn switches to the next player.
  3. 📥 Hold to Save:
    If you want to keep your accumulated current score, click the “HOLD” button to add it to your total score.
  4. 🔄 Switching Turns:
    After holding or rolling a 1, the turn automatically passes to the other player.
  5. 🏆 Win Condition:
    The first player to reach 20 points in total wins the match.
  6. ♻️ New Game:
    Click the “NEW GAME” button at any time to reset the scores and start a new match.
